*** Update regarding manuscript ***

ANSYS _can_ use the mesh of one design in another design.

Status of the ANSYS simulations regarding the momentum simulations:

The open space integrals do not apply with IABC, and the finite space integrals can be done as follows:

* V_M: a new file was added showing the angular momentum calculation with results from both V and M simulations
 
* rho_M, sigma_M, rho_J and sigma_K: these can actually be done in the magnetic simulations (no mixed E&M is required) since rho and sigma are imposed and such values can be used directly in postprocessing (only A is needed). These results are TODO (in progress).

* P_M: can't be done since permanent polarization can't be specified in ANSYS Maxwell

Adding these results to table V is planned for the next revision of the manuscript
