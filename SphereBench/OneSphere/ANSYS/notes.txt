ANSYS can use the mesh of one design in another design within the same AEDT project.

Regarding the finite space momentum integrals (open space integrals do not apply with IABC):

* V_M: shows the angular momentum calculation with results from both V and M simulations in the same project
 
* rho_M, sigma_M, rho_J and sigma_K: these can actually be done in the magnetic simulations (M, J, K) since rho and sigma are imposed and such values can be used directly in postprocessing (only A is needed)

* P_M: can't be done since permanent electric polarization can't be specified in ANSYS Maxwell
